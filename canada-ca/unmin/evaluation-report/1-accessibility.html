Subject
Evaluate the accessibility conformity of the new GCWeb look version 2.
Notes:
It was assumed that GCWeb, released with v4.0.29, was already meeting WCAG 2.0 Level AA. So a brief evaluation was done for those succes criteria. The following evaluation are mainly focus on the new GCWeb look (V2) area of changes.
