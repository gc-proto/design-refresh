What these benefits offer

Employment Insurance maternity and parental benefits provide financial assistance to:

  • a person who is on leave from work because they're pregnant or have recently given birth
  • parents who are on leave to care for a newborn or newly adopted child

You could receive up to 55% of your earnings, to a maximum of $547 a week.

Residents of Quebec

The Province of Quebec is responsible for providing maternity, paternity, parental and adoption benefits to its residents through the Quebec Parental Insurance Program.

Maternity benefits

Maternity benefits are only available to the person giving birth. They cannot be shared between parents.

The person receiving maternity benefits may also be entitled to parental benefits.

Parental benefits

Parents can share these benefits. If sharing each parent must select the same option when submitting their application.

You must choose between 2 options:

  • Standard parental
  • Extended parental

This choice determines the number of weeks and the amount of money you'll receive. Once you start receiving benefits, you cannot change options.

Benefit overview

Benefit name Maximum weeks Benefit rate Weekly max
Maternity (for the person giving birth) up to 15 weeks 55% up to $547
Maternity benefits can transition into parental benefits. You can apply for both at once.
Benefit name Maximum weeks Benefit rate Weekly max
Standard parental up to 40 weeks, but no one parent can receive more than 35 weeks of standard benefits 55% up to $547
Extended parental up to 69 weeks, but no one parent can receive more than 61 weeks of extended benefits 33% up to $328
If sharing, each parent must select the same option when submitting their application.

Examples of options available to families

Maternity plus standard parental

Maria is taking time off work to recover and care for her newborn. She will be sharing parental benefits with her partner. She takes the maximum:

  • 15 weeks maternity
  • 35 weeks of standard parental
  • 50 weeks total for Maria

Her partner can apply for up to 5 weeks of standard parental benefits to care for the baby.

If Maria chooses to use fewer weeks, her partner can apply for more.

Maternity plus extended parental

Janelle is taking time off work to recover and care for her newborn. She will be sharing extended parental benefits with her partner. She takes the maximum:

  • 15 weeks maternity
  • 61 weeks of extended parental
  • 76 weeks total for Janelle

Her partner can apply for up to 8 weeks of extended parental benefits to care for the baby.

If Janelle chooses to use fewer weeks, her partner can apply for more.
