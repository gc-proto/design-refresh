Standard paragraphs

The line spacing is slightly larger than typical, but there is evidence to support that it's easier to read this way.

The problem comes from the spacing between paragraphs not being sufficiently differentiated. If you can't tell if something is a new paragraph or not then it's likely to actually hurt readability. Issues with heading spacing

To that end, we need to increase the space between typical paragraphs. We have to account for spacing under headings as well though - if that's less than the line height then it looks incorrect.

p { margin-bottom: 1.1em; } h2, h3, h4 { margin-bottom: .7em; }

Paragraphs with lists

Pages often have paragraphs with lists mixed in. Short lists can be spaced just like normal lines.

Get everything you need to calculate your income and support any credits, deductions and expenses you’ll claim.

If you were employed or had an investment income in 2018, your employer or financial institution will send you statements commonly referred to as ‘’slips’’. Here are some common examples:

  • T3 Statement of Trust Income Allocation and Designations
  • T4 Statement of Remuneration Paid
  • T5 Statement of Investment Income

Space between the list items is identical to that between normal lines. There's more space before the list than after because the margin is put after the p. So, we need to add a bit of space to the last list item to accommodate it.

ul { margin-bottom: 1.33em; }

Multi-line lists

Short lists work fine, but what if there are multi-line lists? They can seem like they should have more spacing between them.

  • The products marked with (AT) are compatible with Assistive Technology such as Magic and Zoomtext.
  • The "Services Available" column indicates this certified software product has been approved by the CRA to include new forms/functions provided within this product, such as Auto-fill my return .
  • It is your responsibility to research the provider's privacy policies before buying or using a software product or web application.

Each item in that list is basically a paragraph, but because they're list items, there's as much space between the lines as there is between the bullets.
