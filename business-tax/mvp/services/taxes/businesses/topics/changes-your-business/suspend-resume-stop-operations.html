Suspend, resume, or stop operations

When your business temporarily suspends or resumes operations, it is important that you update your business account information with us by calling 1-800-959-5525. We can serve you better if we have your accurate and up-to-date account information.

If your business permanently stops operating, see the topic "Closing accounts"  below. 

Special rules apply if you stop operating because of a receivership or bankruptcy. For more information, see the topic "Receivership/Bankruptcy".
