Historic Sites and Monuments Board of Canada

The mandate of the Historic Sites and Monuments Board of Canada is to advise the Government of Canada, through the Minister of the Environment, on the commemoration of nationally significant aspects of Canada's history.

Designations

Nominations for national site, event, or person of historic significance are reviewed by the Board.

Heritage programs and plans

The Historic Sites and Monuments Board provides advice on the following programs and plans:

  • National historic sites system plan
  • Grave sites of Canadian Prime Ministers
  • Heritage lighthouses
  • Heritage railway stations
  • National cost-sharing program for heritage places
  • National program of historical commemoration
