Find out if you need to charge the GST/HST

Answer a few questions to find out if you need to charge or collect the tax and register to get a GST/HST account number.

Businesses with lower revenue and those that deal in certain goods and services might not need to charge the tax.

Types of real property

  • floating home mooring facilities provided long term
  • lease for exempt re-supply
  • lease of parking space (in connection with residential property)
  • long term or low cost residential rent
  • meal plan in a residential complex
  • residential condominium or co-operative housing common expenses
  • residential condominium parking space
  • sale of farmland to a relative and deemed sales of farmland
  • sale of land under a ground lease
  • sale of residential building to person leasing land
  • sale of residential property after an exempt or deemed taxable supply
  • sale of vacant land and other non-residential property by an individual or a personal trust, in some cases
  • supply of a residential trailer park
  • supply of long term ground lease
  • supply of right to use washing machine or clothes dryer (in connection with residential property)
  • used or occupied residential properties

If the good or service you provide is not in this list, but you fit in this category, call 1-800-959-5525 to check if you might still be exempt from the GST/HST.

Types of health care services

  • acupuncture services
  • ambulance services
  • audiological services
  • chiropodic services
  • chiropractic services
  • dental hygienist service
  • dietetic services
  • food and beverages to a health care facility
  • goods or services covered by provincial health insurance
  • home health care services to include funded personal care services such as bathing, feeding, assistance with dressing, taking medication, cleaning, laundering, meal preparation and child care
  • institutional health care services
  • laboratory, radiological or diagnostic services
  • lease of medical equipment
  • medical and dental services (other than for cosmetic purposes)
  • midwifery services
  • naturopathic services
  • nursing services
  • occupational therapy services
  • optometric services
  • osteopathic services
  • pharmacist consultation and diagnostic request services made after March 29, 2012 (other than drug dispensing services)
  • psychological services
  • physiotherapy services
  • podiatric services
  • purely cosmetic procedures (exempt only if it is necessary for medical or reconstructive purposes or where the procedure is covered under a provincial or territorial health insurance plan)
  • social worker services
  • speech-language pathology services
  • training services or services of designing a training plan that are specially designed to assist individuals with a disorder or disability if the training is not similar to training ordinarily given to individuals who do not have any disorder or disability

If the good or service you provide is not in this list, but you fit in this category, call 1-800-959-5525 to check if you might still be exempt from the GST/HST.

Types of educational services

  • degree granting instructional services and mandatory fees
  • elementary and secondary schooling
  • English and French second language instruction
  • extra-curricular school activities (including food, beverages, services and admissions)
  • food services to universities, colleges and schools
  • lease of goods to students
  • non-degree college and university courses
  • professional accreditation instructional services
  • school cafeteria meals
  • student and teacher services in the course of instruction
  • tutoring and music lessons
  • university and college meal plans
  • vocational course instructional services

If the good or service you provide is not in this list, but you fit in this category, call 1-800-959-5525 to check if you might still be exempt from the GST/HST.

Types of child and personal care services

  • child care services less than 24 hours
  • disability care services
  • residential care services

If the good or service you provide is not in this list, but you fit in this category, call 1-800-959-5525 to check if you might still be exempt from the GST/HST.

Types of legal aid services

  • legal aid services under a provincial legal aid plan

If you are not providing legal aid services under a provincial legal aid plan, but you fit in this category, call 1-800-959-5525 to check if you might still be exempt from the GST/HST.

Types of financial services

  • all supplies of financial services that are not zero-rated
  • deemed financial services

If the good or service you provide is not in this list, but you fit in this category, call 1-800-959-5525 to check if you might still be exempt from the GST/HST.
