2. Eligibility

You can use BRO if you are one of the following:

  • a business owner
  • a third party requester
  • a representative with a RepID or a GroupID
  • an individual that employs a caregiver, a babysitter, or a domestic worker

For more information, go to Authorize a representative – Business number and Employing a caregiver, babysitter, or domestic worker.

Conditions you need to meet

You can use Business Registration Online (BRO) if you have all of the following:

  • valid social insurance number (SIN)
  • income tax return filed with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA)

If you do not have a social insurance number, go to Social Insurance number – Overview.

You can find out if you have to file a tax return and how to get, complete, and send a return at All about your tax return.

Restrictions you need to know

You cannot use BRO if you have any of the following:

  • invalid SIN
  • income tax return was never filed with the CRA
  • GST/HST program account to register for:
    • a business located in Quebec
    • a non-resident business
  • bare trust to register
  • corporation income tax account to register for a business incorporated:
    • federally with Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada
    • provincially in:
      • British Columbia
      • Manitoba
      • New Brunswick
      • Nova Scotia
      • Ontario
      • Saskatchewan

Other ways to register if you cannot use BRO

For more information on other methods for registering other than BRO, go to How to get a BN or register for CRA program accounts.

For more information on other methods for registering in the province where your business operates, go to How to register for provincial or other federal programs.
