Import-export program account

Importing or exporting goods

If you import goods into Canada or export or sell goods in other countries, you need to register for an import-export program account.

We will use your import-export program account number to process customs documents.

To avoid delays in releasing your goods at the border, open your program account before you import or export goods.

When you fill in Form RC1, include all your business's names that may appear on customs release forms and documents, such as invoices. If the name on the customs release document is different from the name we have on file, your goods may be held up at the border.

Creating a new corporation

When two or more corporations merge and create a new corporation, we may issue a new business number to the new corporation. We will immediately close all import-export program accounts you had for the merged corporations.

Obligations for imports and exports

You may have more obligations when importing or exporting goods or services:

  • reporting your shipments
  • accounting for your goods
  • paying taxes
  • paying duty

For more information, go to Import and Export.
