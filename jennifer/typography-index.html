Typographic needs of Canada.ca

While fonts selection are often a matter of personal tastes, there are many choices we can make to ensure readabily.

What we can control:

  • bigger text is better
  • embrace white space (passive and active)
  • line height of 1.5-2x (depending on font)
  • make it easy to distinguish headers and bold text apart from body copy

Font specific choices:

  • large x height helps
  • distinct characters, LI1, 0 vs O
  • no unsual characters or alignments that create readability problems (old style figures for example)
  • enough weights/styles to create contrast
  • look for serif or san-serif fonts optimized for web
  • multi-language character support

Our first exploration

Open Sans was added to our prototypes for optimization projects as an experiment in late 2017. We have collected data for 4 projects so far.

I've made additional modifications for our design refresh.

Open Sans

Pros:

  • good contrast between headers/bold and body copy
  • approx. 65 characters line length in sample layout
  • comes in 10 styles
  • 0 vs O is distinct

Potential cons:

  • relying on one font alone means creating visual contrast between text and headers is a matter of size and weight only
  • not a very distinct LI1

Additional layouts

Lato (h) and Lora (body)

Pros:

  • excellent contrast between headers/bold and body copy
    • contrast is further helped by having a san-serif header with a serif body
  • Lora (body) was designed and optimized for screen appearance
    • passes LI1 and 0 vs O
    • is a serif with lining figures (instead of old style which is harder to read)
    • approx. 70 characters line length in sample layout
  • Lato (headers) is strong with balanced characters (classical proportions)
    • comes in 10 styles
    • passes 0 vs O
    • because of font structure, the font size can be reduced without losing contrast against body type, allowing for headers to occupy less line length

Potential cons:

  • Lora has only 4 styles
  • Lato technically passes LI1, but it could be more obvious

Fira (h and body)

Pros:

  • Fira passes LI1 and 0 vs O
  • approx. 60 characters line length in sample layout
  • Fira comes in 18 styles
  • Fira is designed for a large range of varying screen quality/rendering

Potential cons:
